Chili Con Carne

Description

"One-pot", easy, full of nutrients and protein. Body-building essential.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Red Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Chili Powder
  • Paprika
  • Minced meat - preferably beef, but I like mixed with pork too
  • Tomato pur√©e
  • A can of red kidney beans
  • A side - rice, tortillas, whatever you like

Steps

  1. Prepare all your vegetables, cut into small pieces.
  2. Pot on the medium heat, add a tablespoon of oil, add the onion, stir well, after a few minutes, add all the other vegetables
  3. Turn up the heat and put in the meat. Break it up well with a spatula or spoon. Keep stiring.
  4. Once all the pieces of meat are browned and broke down, add the can of tomatoes, salt, black pepper, chili, whatever spices your kitchen provides and you like.
  5. Once it's gently bubbling, turn down the heat to low and let it do its thing for about 20 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  6. Prepare your side dish in the meanwhile, bought tortillas are really convenient, cause all they need is heating up a bit.
  7. And you are done! Enjoy.
