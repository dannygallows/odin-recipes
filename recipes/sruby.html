Sruby

Description

This is my favorite "one-pot" recipe, that just makes sense and is so easy and quick to do. You will need some high-fat Quark, a traditional dairy product in Slavic speaking countries.

Ingredients

  • fusilli pasta (it looks like a screw, in Slovak language: Sruby)
  • 200 grams of quark
  • 60 grams of bacon
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted boild water, follow the instructions on the package.
  2. While cooking, cut onion and bacon to small pieces.
  3. Once pasta is cooked, leave it in colander.
  4. In the same pot on low heat, add olive oil and onion, stir well. Once it catches a bit of color, throw in the bacon.
  5. Once browned a little, turn off the heat, put in the cooked pasta and stir everything thoroughly.
  6. Finally, crumble the quark onto the pasta and stir again.
